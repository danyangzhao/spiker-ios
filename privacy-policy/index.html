Spikers

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

Spikers ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Spikeball session and game tracker built for groups who love roundnet. We take your privacy seriously and want to be upfront about how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Spikers, we may collect the following information:

  • Player profiles: Name and emoji you choose when creating a player.
  • Game data: Session details, game scores, team compositions, and related statistics.
  • RSVP responses: Your attendance status for upcoming sessions.
  • Device token: If you enable push notifications, we collect your device token solely to send you session-related notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core app functionality — tracking sessions, games, players, and stats.
  • Send push notifications about sessions (only if you opt in).
  • Calculate leaderboards, player statistics, and awards.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Spikers uses Apple Push Notification service (APNs) to deliver notifications to your device. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. If you would like your data to be deleted, please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Spikers is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
